Chocolate Mousse

Mouth watering Chocolate-Mousse

Chocolate mousse is a luxurious and indulgent dessert known for its silky-smooth texture and rich, chocolaty flavor. It's a velvety delight created by whipping together melted chocolate, whipped cream, and beaten egg whites. The result is a light and airy dessert that practically melts in your mouth. Whether served in elegant glasses, as a cake filling, or on its own, chocolate mousse is a classic treat that's beloved by dessert enthusiasts around the world

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces (170g) semisweet or bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Optional: Whipped cream and chocolate shavings for garnish

Steps!

  1. Step 1: Melt Chocolate and Butter
    In a heatproof bowl, combine the chopped chocolate and unsalted butter. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) or microwave in 20-second intervals, stirring until the chocolate and butter are completely melted and smooth. Allow it to cool slightly.
  2. Step 2: Whip the Egg Whites
    In a separate bowl, beat the egg whites with an electric mixer until soft peaks form. Gradually add half of the granulated sugar while continuing to beat. Beat until stiff, glossy peaks form.
  3. Step 3: Make the Chocolate Mixture
    In the bowl with the melted chocolate and butter, whisk in the egg yolks and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Step 4: Combine the Mixtures
    Gently fold one-third of the whipped egg whites into the chocolate mixture to lighten it. Carefully fold in the remaining egg whites until no white streaks remain, being gentle to maintain the mousse's airy texture.
  5. Whip the Cream
    In another bowl, whip the heavy cream until it reaches stiff peaks. Fold the whipped cream into the chocolate mixture until well combined.
  6. Chill and Serve
    Spoon the chocolate mousse into serving glasses or bowls. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until set.
  7. Garnish and Enjoy
    Before serving, you can add a dollop of whipped cream and a sprinkle of chocolate shavings as a garnish, if desired.
